Farmdustry

This mod brings farming to Mindustry :

  • Plant Growers e.g. A carrot grower
  • Processing of goods : e.g. a potato oven
  • Selling of goods : distribution centres
  • Money : money is produced by selling goods and can be used in a limited resources game to build all of the farm equipment
